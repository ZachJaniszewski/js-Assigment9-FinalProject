Welcome to ZAD's Pizza Truck!

ZAD's aims to be Wisconsin's best food truck. We have a policy of only using the freshest and best ingredients in all our menu items. We also only use locally sourced foods and products in our dishes. Some of the things that set our pizza apart: using Caputo 00 flour, Grande mozzarella and San Marzano tomatoes. We also bake our pizzas in our woodfire oven using fresh cut cherry wood from northern Wisconsin that will keep you coming back for more!
